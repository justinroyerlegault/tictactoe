Rules for Tic-Tac-Toe

  1. You play on a 3 X 3 grid.
  2. First player is X, second player is O. Players click turn by turn a case in the grid.
  3. The first player who puts 3 consecutive marks on a row (horizontally, vertically or diagonallly) wins the game.
  4. If the 9 squares are marked and no one has made a row, it's a tie.
